Egg Salad Sandwich

Description

Delicious egg salad for sandwiches. Makes a great lunch time snack

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Mayonnaise
  • Chopped green onion
  • Yellow mustard
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Boil the water and remove from heat. Cover and let eggs sit in hot water for roughly 10 minutes. Remove from water then peel and chop
  2. Place chopped eggs in a bowl; stir in green onion, mayonnaise and mustard. Season with paprika, salt and pepper
  3. Stir and serve on bread
