Chicken Salad

Description

This recipe is healthy, uses simple ingredients and only takes 10 minutes to make!

Ingredients

  • Almonds
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lemon juice
  • Black pepper
  • Cooked chicken
  • Celery

Steps:

  1. Place almonds in frying pan and gently toast over medium heat
  2. Mix mayo, pepper and lemon juice in a bowl. Toss with chicken, celery and the toasted almonds
